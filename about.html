What makes us different?

A focus on excellence in everything we do...

Elite tuition:

  • Experienced, innovative and simply the best A level maths teachers.
  • Years of expertise teaching AQA, Edexcel, OCR, WJEC qualifications.
  • Focusing on problem solving to develop resilience when tackling the question.
  • An understanding that people learn in different ways.

A true focus on body and mind:

  • Developing study habits to ensure continued success and confidence.
  • A technology free day, with a healthy break from mobile phones
  • An emphasis on nutrition and relaxation techniques to cope with exam stress and planning for revision.
  • Mindfulness, yoga and one-one coaching sessions throughout the week.

A 'golden circle' approach to revision sessions:

  • Diagnostic testing before the course to allow for personalised interventions.
  • A confidence building range of one-one sessions, small group tuition and exam board trained examiner masterclasses.
  • Developing peer to peer explanations and support, a sense of community and most importantly enjoyment.

On-going support and staying in touch:

  • Post course package with coaching conversations and direct Maths support where requested.
  • A peer network that allows to continue to build the confidence to unleash your potential.
  • High quality ‘How To’ A-Level Maths tutorial available via our online platform.
  • Part of a community which is always there for you.
